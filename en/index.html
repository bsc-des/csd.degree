Studies take place at Jerusalem's top academic institutions, with Computer Science at the Hebrew University and design at one of Bezalel Academy's various departments. text: >- ## Program Structure A 5-6 years program, in which the first 2 years are focused on Computer Science courses from the Hebrew university, combined with courses that are relevant for all majors of design, from Bezalel. Afterwards, the focus shifts to design, for another 3-4 years, depending on the major in Bezalel academy. In the final year of the program the students will create a large scale project that demonstrates their capabilities. huji: >+ ## Hebrew University Computer Science Department The School of Computer Science & Engineering at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning and research. Leading groundbreaking and revolutionary international studies in science, technology and other areas of scholarship which make a difference globally. bezalel: >- ## Bezalel Academy of Art and Design Established in 1906, Bezalel has evolved into one of Israel's most prestigious art schools. Bezalel's departments include: Fine Arts, Architecture, Ceramics and Glass Design, Industrial Design, Jewelry and Fashion Design, Photography and Visual Communication. Each department preserves traditional knowledge and tools while embracing the use of new technologies. ---
